Modules

Personal Branding Workshop

  • Discover LinkedIn as the #1 platform for personal branding.
  • Learn how the LinkedIn algorithm works and effective strategies to connect with companies and recruiters.
  • Get tips on creating a standout professional profile and showcasing your skills.
  • Learn how to expand your network and connect with other professionals of the industry.

Employer Branding

  • You will learn: The fundamentals of employer branding and its impact on recruitment and retention.
  • You will learn: How to develop a unique employee value proposition (EVP) that attracts top talent.
  • You will learn: Best practices for promoting your company’s culture and values to the outside world.
  • Project: Build an employer branding strategy for a company of your choice.

Recruitment and Selection

  • You will learn: The complete recruitment process, from job analysis to the selection of candidates.
  • You will learn: How to create effective job descriptions and advertisements to attract qualified candidates.
  • You will learn: Screening, interviewing techniques, and strategies for selecting the right talent.
  • Project: Develop a comprehensive recruitment plan for a specific job offering.

Performance Management

  • You will learn: How to set clear performance objectives aligned with company goals.
  • You will learn: Techniques for providing constructive feedback and conducting performance reviews.
  • You will learn: How to design a reward system to motivate and retain high performers.
  • Project: Develop a performance management plan for a specific job or department.

Legal Aspects of HR

  • You will learn: The key legal frameworks related to employment, including hiring, firing, and workplace regulations.
  • You will learn: How to handle employment contracts, benefits, and termination according to the law.
  • You will learn: How to mitigate legal risks by ensuring compliance with labor laws.
  • Project: Quiz assessment on employment legislation.

Financial Aspects of HR

  • You will learn: How to manage HR data and implement financial strategies for compensation and benefits.
  • You will learn: Key methods for budgeting, forecasting, and reporting within the HR department.
  • You will learn: How to design pay structures and identify cost-saving opportunities in HR.
  • Project: Quiz assessment on HR finance and data management.

Training and Development

  • You will learn: How to assess training needs and align them with organizational goals.
  • You will learn: Techniques for designing, delivering, and evaluating training programs.
  • You will learn: Employee development strategies that foster continuous growth and skill enhancement.
  • Project: Design and evaluate a training and development program for employees.

Employee Engagement

  • You will learn: How to measure employee engagement and improve workplace satisfaction.
  • You will learn: Techniques for building a strong company culture and improving team collaboration.
  • You will learn: Methods for boosting employee well-being, productivity, and retention.
  • Project: Prepare and analyze an employee engagement survey, summarizing key insights.

HR Business Partner

  • You will learn: The role of an HR Business Partner and how they align HR strategies with business objectives.
  • You will learn: How to build strong partnerships with business leaders to drive organizational success and address key people challenges.
  • You will learn: Best practices for influencing business decisions through strategic HR initiatives and data-driven insights.
  • Project: Develop an HR business partner strategy for a company of your choice.

HR Strategy

  • You will learn: How to design a comprehensive annual HR strategy that supports company goals.
  • You will learn: Methods for implementing, monitoring, and adjusting the HR strategy to meet business objectives.
  • You will learn: How to measure the impact of HR initiatives on business performance and revenue.
  • Capstone Project: Write an annual HR strategy for a company based on a brief from the CEO.

It is not mandatory that you have previously encountered the tools that will be learned, but it is desirable that you have knowledge of the English language.
